What we do

Flagstaff Adventures provides guided hiking and biking tours of Northern Arizona's beautiful landscapes. Whether backpacking through Oak Creek, biking around the San Francisco peaks, or going for a day hike up Mount Elden, Flagstaff Adventures will take you on a safe, extraordinary trip.

Our goal

We want to share the beauty and majesty of the Colorado Plateau. Let us show you the sunrise over cinder cones, and the sunset over Coconino National Forest. It's our pride and joy to bring the enriching experiences of Flagstaff's landscapes into your life.

Who we are

We're Flagstaff locals with a passion for the outdoors. We're mountain bikers, climbers, hikers, bikepackers and backpackers. We're also poets, photographers, thinkers and friends. We believe a view hiked to is better than a view driven to, and that watching the sun rise over the land is the best way to spend a morning.
